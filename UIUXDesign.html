Touch Service Community

Brief description of the company

Since their registration in 1992, Touch Community Services has been a member of the National Council of Social Services. They have established a forward-thinking community in which everyone is respected and empowered. They have reached out to people from all walks of life over the years, including young people, families, people with special needs, and the elderly. They began in 1986 when a gathering of workers went to the area in Clementi and Jurong to assist the offspring of families with single parent and low pay. They make every effort to keep those in their care from being abandoned.



Describe the app’s purposes

The purpose of the app is to teach skills to the elderly whereby they can use in their daily life. For this app, I will design some food recipe with different difficulty level and the way of going to the supermarket to buy the ingredients with the help of the Live camera. I will also design some pop quiz regarding the steps of making the food to strengthen their memory . Add a calendar whereby it will show when the target audience use the app and their feelings/feedback.

FLy With Me

Features of the App:

It allows user to buy tickets, provides information to users about travel destinations, flight information, and general information about the airline, allows ordering of food during flights at an extra charge, given that no food was ordered pre-boarding



UI Design Concept

Team’s Branding: we decided to adapt a colour scheme similar to Singapore Airlines, and the logo differs from Singapore Airlines' logo, but we wanted to convey a message to the audience: everyone can fly together with convenience and a peace of mind.

Demographic of your target audience: most passengers travel in groups, either as families or friends, the age range can vary greatly, but most are from ages 21 to 60. The majority take economy class, with a good portion taking business class, and the rest lie within other classes.
